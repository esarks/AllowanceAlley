# Option A — Replace App Entry with Standalone Rescue Root
1) Drag BOTH files from `OptionA_ReplaceAppEntry` into Xcode.
2) When prompted for `AllowanceAlleyApp.swift`, choose **Replace**.
3) Product → **Clean Build Folder** (⇧⌘K) → **Run**.

You will see **Dashboard (Rescue) / Chores (Rescue) / Rewards (Rescue)** immediately.
This proves your target/scheme and entry point are correct.
After confirming, you can switch the WindowGroup back to your BRD root later.

# Option B — Keep Your Root, Forward Your Tabs
1) Open your TabView (usually `ContentView.swift`) and note which view types the Chores and Rewards tabs use.
2) From this folder, drag the matching file(s) into Xcode and **Replace** your existing file(s).
   Examples:
   - If your tab shows `ChoresView()`, replace with `OptionB_ForwardTabs/ChoresView.swift`.
   - If it shows `RewardsListView()`, replace with that forwarder.
3) Ensure **Target Membership → AllowanceAlley** is checked.
4) Product → Clean Build Folder (⇧⌘K) → Run.
